## Personal-Data Safety

Do not commit original certificates, identity documents or screenshots containing:

- CPF, TFN or tax identifiers;
- passport or driver's licence numbers;
- home address;
- signatures;
- document verification codes;
- visa documentation;
- employee names, employee IDs or private HR case details.

Only publish the portrait, resume and cover letter after confirming they are intentionally public.

## Sample Content Rules

The sample pack must never include:

- live employee records;
- real staff names or IDs;
- exact dates from private cases;
- screenshots of HR systems;
- disciplinary or investigation facts;
- internal employer templates unless permission exists.

Use generalised workflows, fictional examples and aggregated descriptions.
