The email address appears in both HTML pages. Search the project for the old address before publishing any replacement.

## Personal-Data Safety

Do not commit original certificates, identity documents or screenshots containing:

- CPF or tax identifiers;
- passport or driver's licence numbers;
- home address;
- signatures;
- document verification codes;
- visa documentation.

Only publish the portrait and CV after checking that both are intentionally public.

## Final Review Checklist

- Professional claims remain accurate.
- Illustrative data remains clearly labelled.
- The CV link works.
- Images load correctly.
- No private document has been added.
- The site works on mobile and desktop.
- Spelling and dates match the submitted application.
